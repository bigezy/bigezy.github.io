Our next meeting will be from 3:00 to 5:30 on the Saturday 2/17 with a change in venue. We are at the Urbana Makerspace, 202 West Broadway, Urbana, Illinois. This month we start off at 3pm. As always the format has adjusted a little in response to what worked last month.

This Months Presentations

StyroPyro: A Laser Demonstration
Do you like lasers? Did you ever want to see an ACTUAL LASER (zomg) pop a balloon, light a match, burn through plastic, or create supreme razzle-dazzle patterns on your ceiling? This demo is for you! DC217 will be featuring a laser demonstration with some basic information on wavelength and power! Safety wear for your seeing-orbs will be required! If you have a pair of laser-safe goggles, bring them, but we may have spares. Laser-cat says pew-pew! (●ↀωↀ●)

Virtualization, Emulation, Simulation and other Similar Sounding Semi-Synonym
 Is simulation the same as emulation? How does virtualization fit in? What the heck are Docker Containers?
Why should I care? Shane McFly talks about the nuances that separate VMs and Containers and more.

Breakout Sessions After the talks conclude we will do "bring us your computer problems" and break into groups. Any questions or typo's please send email to info at dc217.org.
